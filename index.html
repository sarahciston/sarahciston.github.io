Sarah Ciston (she/they) is a computational media artist and experimental writer named one of San Francisco Weekly's Best Writers Without a Book. They hold an MFA in Hybrid Writing from UC San Diego and are a Mellon PhD Fellow and an Annenberg Fellow in Media Arts + Practice at University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts, where she organizes Creative Code Collective--a community of students learning computational media in an engaging, non-intimidating environment that values project-driven approaches and creative criticality. They are also a virtual fellow at the Humboldt Institute for Internet and Society in Berlin.
